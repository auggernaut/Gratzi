Gratzi

Thank the people in your life.

Create a Gratzi

Distributed

This application can run anywhere, even on your local machine. Download, modify, and run it however you like.

Personal

Every Gratzi you create is stored in your DropBox, Google Drive, GitHub, or wherever you have cloud storage. There is no big server somewhere storing your Gratzi.

Open

Anyone can change how Gratzi works or create new tools for it. Since no single organization owns this (see distributed and personal) it's free to evolve organically.
